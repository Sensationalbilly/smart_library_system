🛡️ AI-Powered Fraud Detection

Smart Financial Shield uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze your transactions in real-time. Our system examines multiple risk factors to calculate a comprehensive fraud risk score, helping protect you from financial fraud before it happens.

Risk Factors We Analyze

💰

Transaction Amount

Large transactions carry higher fraud risk. We analyze the amount and compare it to your typical spending patterns.

• $1,000-$5,000: +15 points
• $5,000-$10,000: +25 points
• Over $10,000: +40 points
🌍

Transaction Type

International transactions have statistically higher fraud rates due to cross-border complexities and less oversight.

• International: +30 points
• Domestic: 0 points
🕐

Time of Transaction

Fraudsters often operate during unusual hours when victims are less likely to notice. Late-night transactions are flagged.

• 12 AM - 6 AM: +20 points
• Normal hours: 0 points
⚡

Transaction Frequency

Multiple transactions in a short time period can indicate account compromise or card testing by fraudsters.

• 3+ transactions/hour: +25 points
• Normal frequency: 0 points
🔢

Amount Patterns

Round number amounts (like $1,000, $5,000) are common in fraud as criminals often test with even amounts.

• Round amounts ≥$1,000: +10 points
• Irregular amounts: 0 points
📊

Historical Behavior

We compare current transactions against your historical patterns to detect anomalies and unusual activity.

• Deviation from normal: Variable points
• Consistent pattern: 0 points

⚙️ How the Detection Process Works

1

Transaction Initiated

When you make a transaction, our system immediately captures all relevant details including amount, type, location, and timestamp.

2

Real-Time Analysis

Our AI engine analyzes the transaction against multiple risk factors in milliseconds. Each factor is weighted based on fraud probability.

3

Risk Score Calculation

All risk factors are combined to produce a comprehensive risk score from 0-100. Higher scores indicate greater fraud likelihood.

4

Decision & Action

Based on the risk score, the system automatically approves, flags for review, or blocks the transaction. You receive instant notifications.

5

Continuous Learning

Our AI learns from every transaction, improving accuracy over time and adapting to new fraud patterns as they emerge.

🎯 Understanding Risk Levels

✅ LOW RISK (0-39 points)

What it means: The transaction appears normal with no significant fraud indicators detected.

Action: Transaction is automatically approved and processed immediately.

Example: $50 domestic transaction during business hours with normal frequency.

⚠️ MEDIUM RISK (40-69 points)

What it means: Some suspicious factors detected, but not conclusive. Requires additional verification.

Action: Transaction is held for review. You'll receive a notification to confirm the transaction.

Example: $3,000 international transaction or multiple transactions in short time.

🚨 HIGH RISK (70-100 points)

What it means: Multiple fraud indicators detected. High probability of fraudulent activity.

Action: Transaction is automatically blocked. You must verify your identity to proceed.

Example: $15,000 international transaction at 3 AM with round amount.
